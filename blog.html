Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to flood relief efforts?

You can donate to flood relief efforts by clicking on the 'Donate' button on our homepage. You can choose the amount and method of payment, including credit card, bank transfer, or mobile banking services.

Where does my donation go?

Your donation will be used to provide immediate relief to flood victims, including food, water, medical aid, and shelter. We work with local partners to ensure the funds are allocated where they are needed most.

Can I track the impact of my donation?

Yes, once you donate, you will receive updates on how your contribution is being used. You can also visit our 'Impact' page to see the number of people helped and resources distributed through your generosity.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, donations made to our platform are tax-deductible. After completing your donation, you will receive a receipt via email that you can use for tax purposes.

How can I volunteer or get involved beyond donations?

In addition to donating, you can volunteer with us by joining local efforts in the affected areas. Visit our 'Get Involved' page to find out how you can support our on-the-ground efforts.
